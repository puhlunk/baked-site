Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cakes and ordering process

Do you offer delivery?

We currently offer pickup only from our 8th Avenue South location.

How far in advance should I order?

Same-day orders are welcome, but for custom designs, we recommend ordering at least 48 hours in advance.

Do you make custom designs?

Yes! Please call us at (615) 933-1100 to discuss custom cake and cookie options.

Do you accommodate allergies?

We can accommodate some dietary restrictions, but our kitchen does contain common allergens. Please call to discuss your specific needs.

How should I store my cake?

For best freshness, store your cake in the refrigerator. Let it sit at room temperature for 20-30 minutes before serving.

What size are your cakes?

Our standard cakes serve 8-10 people. Larger sizes are available for custom orders.
